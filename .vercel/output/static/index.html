So, what exactly is nearwork?

We humans are social beings, and we like to belong, have fun and enjoy experiences together. Remote work has caused a major decline in this behavior in spite of it being logically convenient for everything else. This is what we are trying to solve, to help you stay productive and social while you make the most out of living your remote and free life.
